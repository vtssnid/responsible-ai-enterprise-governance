# Responsible AI Governance Lifecycle

## Prepare
- Use-case definition and ethical intent assessment
- Data sourcing, consent, and usage validation
- Initial risk classification (low / medium / high impact)
- Approval checkpoints prior to development

## Validate
- Model performance and robustness testing
- Bias and fairness assessment
- Explainability and transparency validation
- Security and access control review

## Serve
- Controlled deployment into production environments
- Human-in-the-loop decision mechanisms
- Continuous monitoring for drift and unintended outcomes
- Incident response and rollback procedures
